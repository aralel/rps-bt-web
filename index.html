Rock Paper Bluetooth is a fast, face-to-face Rock Paper Scissors game for nearby players. No account needed. Host a game, join a game, and settle it in real time.

Why players use Rock Paper Bluetooth

Real-time local play

Connect to nearby players over Bluetooth and start rounds quickly without creating an account.

Simple match flow

Host or find a game, pick your move, and play first-to-score matches with rematch support.

Match history

Review outcomes, scores, and round details to track wins, losses, and your best sessions.

How it works

1. Set nickname

Pick the name nearby players will see.

2. Host or Join

One player hosts, the other finds and connects.

3. Select move

Choose rock, paper, or scissors before reveal.

4. Win the match

Reach the target score first and request a rematch.
